# Purpose

This document stores ideas.

Ideas do not automatically become roadmap items.

Ideas are collected here for future evaluation.

# Rules

1. Every idea is welcome.

2. No idea is implemented immediately.

3. Ideas must survive review before entering the roadmap.

4. The existence of an idea does not mean it should be built.
